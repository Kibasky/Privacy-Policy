5. Do We Use Analytics and Tracking Technologies?

In Short: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how our app is used. Multiversia is a native Android application and does not use cookies or web-based tracking technologies.

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymised usage data about how users interact with the app. This includes information such as which features are used, session duration, device type, and country. This data helps us improve the app experience.

We do not use cookies, web beacons, pixels, or any web-based advertising technologies. We do not serve targeted advertising, sell user data, or share usage data with advertising platforms.

Firebase Analytics

We share anonymised usage data with Google Firebase to analyse the use of the Services. For more information on the privacy practices of Google, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms page. Firebase Analytics data is retained for a maximum of 14 months by Google.

To learn more about how Firebase handles data, visit Firebase Privacy and Security.

11. Controls for Do-Not-Track Features

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") feature or setting you can activate to signal your privacy preference. At this stage, no uniform technology standard for recognising and implementing DNT signals has been finalised. As such, we do not currently respond to DNT signals or any other mechanism that automatically communicates your choice not to be tracked. If a standard for online tracking is adopted that we must follow in the future, we will inform you about that practice in a revised version of this Privacy Notice.
